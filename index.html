What We Do

Shipbrokering

We support ship sale and purchase transactions, chartering mandates, and newbuilding-related projects across a range of vessel segments and market conditions. Our focus is on disciplined execution, clear positioning, and controlled negotiation.

Advisory & Consulting

We provide strategic and transactional advisory tailored to ownership structures, investment strategies, and operational realities. This includes asset advisory, market intelligence, transaction structuring, and coordination across jurisdictions.

Strategic & Project Support

For complex or non-standard mandates, we assist clients with market entry strategies, fleet positioning, special situations, and high-level negotiations requiring discretion and experience.
